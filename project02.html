Poke Poke APP

Subculture Communicate APP | 03.2016

For this project, we need to choose a subculture and use the visual language which comes from this subculture to make an interesting communicate application. I chose the Pokemon subculture. I decided to use red, yellow and black as dominant hue, and the Pokemon ball as my basic icon. About the concept in this app, I tried to use the reverse thinking. In my app, users will become to special Pokemon, but not the Pokemon's trainer. They can communicate,make new friends and even battle with each other as a Pokemon. Also, the interaction in is app is one thing I really thought a lot. So I designed the "ball open" interaction which match the subculture.
