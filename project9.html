The Future World

Visual Identity, Photography, Storytelling | 08.2016

In the future, in order to reduce the suicide rate, the government invent a medicine which can stop the people from feeling sadness. Because of losing the comparison, people cannot feel sadness means they also cannot feel happiness too. They don’t care everything and become more and more similar. They start to eat nonsense food, wear same clothes and follow the same schedule. In this future, people believe this is the most happy world than ever, but the truth is on the other hand.

Objective: For this project, I create a imaginary world and design the whole visual identity which even includes a lot of models for the real things in this world. I want to use the visual language and the social rules to show what is this world really looks like. I choose monotone, gray, repetition as my keywords. The only color in this world is yellow which comes from the medicine. I want to use the contrast between gray and yellow to show how ironic this world is.
