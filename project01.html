DanCing Yun / 丹青 韵

Product Design | 05.2014

DanCing is the name of a color in Chinese painting and a kind of glazing color in the ceramics. So I use this as my product's name to represent the combine of ceramic and hinese painting. At same time, I use cloth and wood as the material to make the background and shelves showing Chinese style. In this design, all items were made by myself including wood holder, porcelain and painting.
