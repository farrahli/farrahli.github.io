Visual Essay

Book Design, Binding, Typography | 05.2016

I create a book as my visual essay to proof "paper media will not disappear during millennials". Before I making this book, I did a lot of research and sketch. I tried many different media and found that using paper is the best way for my project. I also create a system, not in typography but also the visual language which I used. I decided to use two contrast voice in my book, the white paper part means the people who think paper media will disappear, and the yellow paper part is the adverse voice, depending on the date of my research, shows it will not disappear. Also, it following the function as the post-it note. And I tried to use a different way to show the contrast of the paper media and the digital media.

All of the research is here: http://farrahseminar.tumblr.com/
