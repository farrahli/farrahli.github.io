Love Letter

Book Cover Design | 06.2015

This is a youth campus novel in which everything starts from a paper plant. So I use the hand drawing paper plant as my main character for the cover. I design two different kinds of cover, which mainly in feeling. One is very sweet and the other one is gloomy. They are all from the feeling when I read the book brief introduction. At last, the client choosing the blue one.
