Let's Talk

Poster Design, Visual Identity | 08.2015

The topic of this project is to tell people more focus on the communication between face to face, not just through the digital media. Also, I did some breakthrough in my poster, for example, add some interaction elements. The left poster represents the positive atmosphere when people chat with real people. The right one is that sometimes people will not show their real feeling, or real face through the digital media. And for the last one in the photo, I hope people can play with this poster and leave some message on it.
