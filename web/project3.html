My Inspriation

Schematic, Typography | 07.2016

In this poster, I use three different types of abstract symbol and color to show three different ways about where my ideas come from. They help me to guide the audiences to read my schematic's information. The challenge in this project is that there are to many information I want to put in my schematic. I tried my best to organize them, put them in the poster, and give them a very clear hierarchy. Also, I use hierarchy to show the influence degree of each element.
